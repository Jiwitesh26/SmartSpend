Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is SmartSpend really free?

Yes! Our Freemium plan is completely free forever with basic tracking, 3 budgets, and monthly reports. Premium features cost ‚Çπ149/month with a 14-day free trial.

How does SmartSpend access my UPI transactions?

SmartSpend reads transaction notifications from your device locally or through secure read-only UPI integrations. We never access your UPI PIN, bank passwords, or the ability to make payments.

Which UPI apps are supported?

We support all major UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM, and more. You can also enable SMS parsing as an alternative.

Can I use SmartSpend without connecting my UPI account?

Absolutely! You can manually enter transactions or just enable SMS parsing. The choice is yours.

What happens to my data if I stop using SmartSpend?

You own your data. You can export everything to Excel/CSV and delete your account anytime. We'll permanently remove all your data within 30 days of account deletion.

Does SmartSpend work offline?

Yes! The core features work offline. Cloud sync and advanced insights require internet connection (Premium feature).

How accurate is the auto-categorization?

Our AI categorization is 90%+ accurate and improves over time as you use the app. You can always edit categories manually.

Can I share access with family members?

Currently, each account is individual. Family sharing features are coming soon in a future update!
